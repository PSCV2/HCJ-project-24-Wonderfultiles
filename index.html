Installation

  • INSTALLATION PROCESS

    Wonderful Tiles can be glued to surfaces using most standard laminate adhesives having a thick body or foaming quality. Prior to application of the glue clean, brush and de-grease the receiving surface or dust, oils or any other contaminants. In same installations, and depending upon the adhesive used, it may be necessary to prepare the back of the thin slate stone veneer with solvent or recommended primer by the adhesive manufacture. We recommend making a test area with any adhesive prior to final application.

  • APPLICATIONS OF Wonderful Tiles

    Wonderful Tiles is a stone sheet made of REAL SLATE, sliced thinly using a patented process then infused with a fiberglass backing, giving Wonderful Tiles unique and beneficial characteristics that the original stone never had. Tradition, technology, passion and quality. Wonderful Tiles is all this and much more.In Furniture production – many new design accents through very simple processing and machining. “Stony Highlights” also find access in the automobile industry, train cabins, yachts, elevators etc.

  • CURVATURE & BENDING

    Wonderful Tiles can be bent with same flexibility as any plastic sheet product. The backing used gives it enough strength and flexibility. Thin Slate Stone Veneer can be bent in concave & convex forms, or arched or radial forms depending on the nature of curve.

  • MATERIAL DATA

    Standard Size per Sheet : 1200 mm x 600 mm & 2400 x 1200 mm Average thickness per Sheet : 1.2mm (1-3 mm) Average Weight per Sheet : 1.6 Kgs Available Colors and Textures : 12
